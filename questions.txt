decorator
Decorators are a design pattern in JavaScript that enables the modification of an object’s behavior by adding new functionality to it,
without altering its original code.
EX:Exp. Imports for Decorators: import {Component} from ‘@angular/core’;

annotation
Annotations are used for creating an attribute annotations that stores array.
EX:Imports for Annotations: import {ComponentAnnotation as Component} from ‘@angular/core’;
